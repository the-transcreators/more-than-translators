WEBSITE LOCALIZATION AS EASY AS IT GETS

With website work, we normally just deal with the text. But some customers want more. They are looking for someone to handle the website design and build.

We are not web designers, but we have developed a way to help you quickly get a low-cost English version of your website. We can transcreate your text for you while simultaneously building the English version of your website. The process we have developed allows us to produce a great-looking result for almost no extra charge. This is particularly attractive for small businesses that do not have a huge marketing budget.

Here's what we offer:

  • A straightforward, static website - clean, modern, clear
  • Almost no extra charge due to simplicity - no interactivity, no database, only one template
  • Editable by you (or by us) in the future - no coding, easy-to-use free editor

We will transcreate your copy directly in a simple, free and easy-to-use website builder (that's one reason why the cost is so low)

Working directly in a simple website builder hardly takes any longer than working in Word. So the website-building work only adds a tiny extra cost. We use a free, open source, offline website builder that is not tied to any web hosting service. The user interface is intuitive and does not require any coding.

We always use the same familiar, simple, template (that's another reason why the cost is so low)

Not only are we not website designers, we do not want to become website designers either. And most of our customers don't. So the template we would use for your site is extremely simple. There is no long learning phase required. We use this template the same way every time so that we never end up spending hours trying to change something. We did spent a lot of time at the beginning on developing a template that does not need to be changed in order to produce a website that looks clean, modern and clear.

What you pay for is the cost of transcreating your text. The vast majority of our effort goes into the words. The words are one of the two big things that will make your message powerful. The other big thing is images. You tell us what images you want to include, or add them yourself later.

If you like the philosophy behind our approach and want the benefits, then the English version of your website must be created using the one and only template we use.

This solution is only suitable if it is okay for your English website to look different from your main website.

We will hand over the project to you and you can easily use the free website builder to make changes to your site

When the project is delivered to you, you take control of it. It is an offline project and the editor is in the form of open source, installable, offline software. So you really do have control over your project. And you can keep it secure.

If you want to make changes at any time, you can easily do so yourself for free. If it is weeks or months since the last time you made changes, you will still not have to worry about how to work with the editor. It is so simple that it can be learned or re-learned in minutes. But, if you prefer us to do it, we will, of course, be delighted to help.

You might like the new English version of your website so much that you decide to adopt the same website builder and design for your main version

And why not? It will look great. And the editor is easy for normal humans to use. And it's free. And it's not bundled with a payment plan that you have to pay for month after month, whether or not you are using it to make changes. And you are not tied to any website hosting service.

There is beauty in simplicity.
